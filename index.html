FAQ

  • Where does CGE depart from?
    • The Cartier departs from the Marina Mirage in the Gold Coast canal. The CGE team will be waiting at the private entry to escort you to the Cartier. Make sure you arrive 15 minutes before departure time to allow for boarding.
  • What are the departure times for the events?
    • Currently, we have events departing at 12pm and 6pm. Each event has a maximum capacity, so tickets are non-refundable. Private functions allow for leeway with departure times.
  • Do you only depart from Gold Coast?
    • We only depart from the Gold Coast this year. Due to COVID19, we will only be docking and departing from Marina Mirage. If you would like to book a private function, and would like to dock elsewhere, please contact us for more information.
  • Can I book a Private Function?
    • A private function can be booked and curated to your specifications within reason. You can book a private function by contacting one of our friendly staff here. Only limited spots available.
  • Can I add entertainment extras?
    • We can organise additional entertainment for your private function at an extra cost. Please contact us for more information.
  • Are you COVID-19 safe?
    • We adhere to the strictest standards of hygiene and cleanliness during these trying times and ensure a safe and enjoyable time during your cruise.

FAQ

  • Where does CGE depart from?
    • The Cartier departs from the Marina Mirage in the Gold Coast canal. The CGE team will be waiting at the private entry to escort you to the Cartier. Make sure you arrive 15 minutes before departure time to allow for boarding.
  • What are the departure times for the events?
    • Currently, we have events departing at 12pm and 6pm. Each event has a maximum capacity, so tickets are non-refundable. Private functions allow for leeway with departure times.
  • Do you only depart from Gold Coast?
    • We only depart from the Gold Coast this year. Due to COVID-19, we will only be docking and departing from Marina Mirage. If you would like to book a private function, and would like to dock elsewhere, please contact us for more information.
  • Do you guys have a photographer?
    • We are able to provide a professional photographer/videographer to capture you having the time of your life at a small extra fee. This will depend on the group size and if you require additional photos edited. Ask us for a tailored quote price.
  • Is there an age restriction?
    • Everyone of any age is welcome aboard, although you must have a valid open license in order to book the Cartier Bundle, and over the age of 18 if you wish to consume alcohol.
  • Do you cater for special requirements?
    • We are able to take any dietary and allergy related requirements into consideration prior to your arrival. Disability ramps are also provided for those with a wheelchair. If you have any of these requirements, please message us so that we can prepare the best experience for you.
  • Can I add entertainment extras?
    • We can organise additional entertainment for your private function at an extra cost. Please contact us for more information.
  • Are you COVID-19 safe?
    • We adhere to the strictest standards of hygiene and cleanliness during these trying times and ensure a safe and enjoyable time during your cruise.
